About Us

At Espresso Yourself, we take immense pride in our rich Philippine culture and artistry. Our brand is a vibrant celebration of the deep love we have for our country, reflected in the exceptional coffee and unparalleled service we provide. Founded by Ralph James Brigoli on October 18, 2006, Espresso Yourself stands as a beacon, redefining thelandscape for startup businesses and social enterprises. We are on a mission to shine the global spotlight on the exquisite flavors of Philippine Coffee and preserve the essence of Filipino tradition. Join us in savoring the perfect blend of culture, tradition, and unparalleled coffee experiences at Espresso Yourself.

We put the spotlight on Philippine Coffee. Each cup of coffee sold supports local farmers through fair trade and contains beans sourced across the Philippines from Sagada & Benguet in the North, to Mt. Apo, Mt. Kitanglad, and Mt. Matutum in the South.
